From gentle family floats to adrenaline-pumping rapids, we offer a variety of trips for all skill levels. Explore our options and find your perfect adventure!

What to Bring

  • Quick-dry clothing (avoid cotton)
  • Secure water shoes or sport sandals
  • Sunscreen and hat
  • Sunglasses with retainer strap
  • Water bottle
  • Change of clothes for after the trip
  • Waterproof camera (optional)

All safety equipment including life jackets, helmets, and wetsuits (when necessary) are provided by us.
